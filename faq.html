FAQ

Frequently asked questions about membership & classes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes — we offer a one-day free trial. Contact us to book.

What are your operating hours?

Mon-Sun: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, quarterly and yearly plans can be frozen for medical reasons with documentation.

Do you provide diet plans?

Yes — our certified nutritionists create personalized diet plans based on your goals.

Are personal trainers available?

Yes, we have certified trainers for one-on-one sessions.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes — we provide free parking for all members.

Is there a joining fee?

No — we don’t charge any joining fee for new members.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

Do you have group classes?

Yes, we offer group sessions for yoga, Zumba, and functional training.

Do you offer online workout sessions?

Yes — we conduct virtual classes via Zoom for remote members.

Can I bring a guest?

Yes, guest passes are available for members on certain days.

Do you provide lockers?

Yes — lockers are available to store your belongings safely.

Do you provide workout clothes?

No, members are required to bring their own workout attire.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes — free high-speed Wi-Fi is available for all members.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes — you can cancel anytime, but cancellation policies may apply.

Do you have special discounts?

Yes — we offer discounts for students, senior citizens, and corporate tie-ups.

Do you provide supplements?

Yes — our in-house store sells high-quality supplements.

Do you have child care facilities?

Currently, we don’t have child care facilities.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes — our mobile app lets you track workouts, book classes, and manage your membership.
